Advanced AI Hacks

By AllTricks Team · Jan 1, 2025 · Tags: Advanced Hacks

Systemization

Define explicit roles, goals, guardrails, and feedback loops. Iterate with chain-of-thought alternates and self-critique.

Prompt Chaining

Step 1: Decompose the problem into subproblems and list assumptions.
Step 2: Solve each subproblem with explicit inputs/outputs.
Step 3: Synthesize final answer; include failure modes and tests.

Adversarial Evaluation

Act as a red-team reviewer. Find errors, ambiguity, and missing constraints in the draft. Suggest concrete fixes and test cases.
